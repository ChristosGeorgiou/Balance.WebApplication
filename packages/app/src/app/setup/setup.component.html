Balnc Setup

Balnc (balance) is an free open source management suite for small businesses. It has a collection of pre-build modules and can be easily extended to create new ones.

All modules are running at local user's machine/browser. This improves loading speed to a magnitute similar of the installable applications.

Your data may be configured to also be stored localy. A database server is not required to use Blanc. As so, they are not visible to any third parties and the speed is improved even more.
