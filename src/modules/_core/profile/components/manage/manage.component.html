Welcome to Balance!

Balance is a collection of business modules for managing simple operations of small companies and freelancers.

It includes sales, marketing and accounting tools and it is both open source and free under the MIT license.

Local Profile

If you would like to use Balance in your local machine, without online sync & backup use this option. You may export/import data from other machines.

Remote Profile

If you would like to use a remote account of Balance use this option. You may manage accounts from the menu afterwards.
