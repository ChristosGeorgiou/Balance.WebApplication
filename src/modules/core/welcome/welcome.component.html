Welcome to Balance!

Balance is a collection of business modules for managing simple operations of small companies and freelancers.

It includes sales, marketing and accounting tools and it is both open source and free under the MIT license.

Balance using an internal profile manager for storing localy all your personal information. Losing access to your computer means that you are losing access to you data.

Online sync and backup functionality is available.
